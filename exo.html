7 Day Exercise Program To Stay Fit And Healthy During The Lockdown

1- The lockdown has confined us to our homes on a daily basis for several consecutive weeks, meaning we can’t go and come as we please.

2- We can’t even venture outside the perimeter of of our homes unless we’re seeking essential services.

3- Those who are used to their daily gym regimen, can’t go as well, seeing that all gyms have currently been shut down in attempts to contain the novel coronavirus which has killed over 150 000 people worldwide.

4- Fitness enthusiasts are getting stir crazy, our jeans are starting to feel a little tighter, from all that eating and couch surfing without burning the calories. Not everyone has a home gym with state of the art equipment, so they can work out.

5- In some areas people can’t go for their jogs as well and some governments have been advising people to jog in their homes and work out in their homes like Ugandan President Yoweri Museveni did.

6- We offer you the seven day exercise regimen so that you stay fit, healthy, burn some carbs and be stress free in the process.

7- You don’t have to go to the gym in order to do this and you don’t need equipment either. Just exercising the good, old fashioned way.
