why should you go to the gym


Going to the gym is essential because the human body is designed for movement. Our organs and muscles need action to work correctly. The gym helps us maintain balance and avoid chronic noncommunicable diseases, locomotion diseases, diabetes, blood problems, and fatigue

Gym memberships usually grow especially after the holidays when people eat so much. Then they go to the gym to burn the fat. Going to the gym is a commitment that people should make if they are serious in getting in shape.
